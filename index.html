🔒 Privacy policy

We don't store your data

The bot reads a username, fetches stats from Hypixel, and responds. Nothing is saved, logged, or tracked.

Last updated: June 2026
⚡

In a nutshell — Don't spam the bot or try to break it. Stats are accurate. The service is free and may go down occasionally.

🤖

What the bot does

The Unexpected Stats Viewer fetches and displays Hypixel statistics in Discord. It's a read-only stats tool — it doesn't interact with your Minecraft account, send messages on your behalf, or do anything else.

🚫

Don't do this

  • Spam commands to overload the bot
  • Try to exploit or reverse-engineer it
  • Violate Discord's or Hypixel's own terms of service
⚙️

Service availability

The bot is free to use. It may go offline for maintenance or updates occasionally. We reserve the right to change or discontinue it at any time.

In a nutshell — We only see the Minecraft username you type. We don't store it. It's gone the moment your stats are returned.

What we process

When you run a command, the bot temporarily processes:

  • The Minecraft username or UUID you provide
  • Your Discord server and channel ID — only to know where to send the reply

None of this is stored after the response is sent.

🚫

What we don't collect

  • Your Discord username or profile info
  • Your IP address
  • Any history of past lookups
  • Anything else — seriously
🔗

Third-party APIs

Stats are fetched live from the Hypixel API. Hypixel's and Discord's own privacy policies apply to their respective platforms.
